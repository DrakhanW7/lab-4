PNG Format Example

About This Image

This image is a coffee graphic instead of a full photograph. It has artistic curve lines, modest colors, and a clear background that lets it blend into the webpage design.

This is the kind of image commonly used for logos, stickers, and simple web graphics because it stays sharp and neat around the edges.

Why PNG Format?

PNG works well for this image because it supports transparency and keeps edges sharp to a tea. It is better than JPG for this kind of logo because the graphic needs to look clean and not have a blurry background behind it.
