JPG Format Example

About This Image

This image shows a cup of coffee placed on a table surronded by plants. It looks like a real everyday photograph with natural lighting, really detailed, and nature colors.

I used this image because it is a good example of the kind of realistic photograph often found on websites, blogs, and business pages.

Why JPG Format?

JPG is a good choice for this image because it is a photograph with many colors, plants, and can tell a story just by an image. JPG eats up photo files so efficiently, so it can keep a smaller file size while still looking perfecto on a webpage.
